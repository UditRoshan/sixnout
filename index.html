Our Journey

Founded in 2015, Cricket Unlimited was established with a passion for promoting cricket at the grassroots level and creating platforms where players of all backgrounds can showcase their talent. What began as a small local tournament has now expanded to a series of highly anticipated cricket events across the country, encompassing all major formats of the game – from T20 to One-Day Internationals and Test matches. Over the years, we’ve grown into a recognized name in the cricket community, organizing tournaments that bring together players, fans, and communities who share a deep love for the sport.

Our Mission

At Cricket Unlimited, our mission is simple: to provide a competitive and enjoyable platform for cricketers to hone their skills, engage in healthy competition, and connect with fans. We are committed to nurturing talent, organizing high-quality tournaments, and promoting a spirit of sportsmanship and camaraderie.

What We Do?

We organize tournaments in various formats – T20, One-Day Internationals, and Test matches – across different age groups and skill levels, actively scout and support emerging cricketers, offering them the chance to compete at higher levels and collaborate with local cricket clubs, schools, and youth organizations to foster a love for the game and provide access to competitive cricket for all.
